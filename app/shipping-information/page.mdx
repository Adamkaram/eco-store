# Shipping Information

At Glow Beauty, we strive to deliver your products as quickly and efficiently as possible. Please review our shipping policies below.

## Shipping Methods

1. **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days
2. **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days
3. **Overnight Shipping**: Next business day (order by 2 PM EST)

## Shipping Rates

- Orders over $50: Free standard shipping
- Standard Shipping: $5.99
- Express Shipping: $12.99
- Overnight Shipping: $24.99

## Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer.

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.

## International Shipping

We currently ship to select countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Please check our international shipping page for more details.

## Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have shipping restrictions due to their ingredients or packaging. These will be noted on the product page.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of the expected delivery date.

## Questions?

If you have any questions about our shipping policies or need help with a specific order, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team.

Thank you for shopping with Glow Beauty!
