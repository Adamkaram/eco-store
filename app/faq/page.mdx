# Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ page. Here you'll find answers to the most common questions about Glow Beauty products and services.

## Product Information

### Q: Are your products cruelty-free?
A: Yes, all Glow Beauty products are cruelty-free. We do not test on animals at any stage of product development.

### Q: Are your products vegan?
A: Many of our products are vegan, but not all. Vegan products are clearly labeled on our website and packaging.

### Q: Do you offer samples?
A: Yes, we offer samples with every order. You can select up to 3 free samples at checkout.

## Orders and Shipping

### Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is 2-3 business days. Please see our Shipping Information page for more details.

### Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to select countries. International shipping rates and times vary by location.

### Q: Can I change or cancel my order?
A: You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

## Returns and Exchanges

### Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day return policy for most new, unopened items. Please see our Returns and Exchanges page for full details.

### Q: How do I return an item?
A: You can initiate a return through your account on our website. We'll provide a return shipping label and instructions.

## Account and Privacy

### Q: How do I create an account?
A: You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website.

### Q: Is my personal information secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information.

## Rewards Program

### Q: Do you have a loyalty program?
A: Yes, we have the Glow Rewards program. You earn points on every purchase which can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

### Q: How do I join the rewards program?
A: You're automatically enrolled in our rewards program when you create an account.

If you can't find the answer to your question here, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team. We're here to help!
