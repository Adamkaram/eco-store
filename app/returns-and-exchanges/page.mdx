# Returns and Exchanges

At Glow Beauty, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

## Return Policy

- You may return most new, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
- We'll also pay the return shipping costs if the return is a result of our error (you received an incorrect or defective item, etc.).

## How to Return an Item

1. Log in to your account and go to your order history.
2. Select the item you wish to return and follow the prompts.
3. Print the provided return shipping label.
4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible.
5. Attach the return shipping label to your package.
6. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location.

## Exchanges

If you need a different size, color, or item, the easiest way is to return the original item for a refund and place a new order.

## Refund Timing

- Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item.
- We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item.
- If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment.
- You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

## Non-Returnable Items

Certain items can't be returned, including:

- Gift cards
- Downloadable software products
- Some health and personal care items

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately for assistance.

## Questions?

If you have any questions about our returns policy, please contact our customer service team.

We appreciate your business and hope you enjoy your Glow Beauty products!
